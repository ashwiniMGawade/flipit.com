Frequent asked questions

How does a coupon code work and where can you use it? Find out the answers to your questions here. Learn exactly how to enter a code and get important information about discount codes, the site, newsletters and more.

The operation of discount codes

What is a discount code?

As following you can use a coupon on your website

  • Step 1. Go to the shopping list.
  • Step 2. Click on the shop that you want to place an order in.
  • Step 3. View of the discount code (if available) that you want to use.
  • Step 4. Click on the discount code.
  • Step 5. This automatically opens a pop-up where you will find the discount code.
  • Step 6. Enter this discount code at the shop during the ordering process.
